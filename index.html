01 · about

a little about me

I'm a final-year B.Tech Computer Science graduate with a minor in Health Informatics, and an aspiring frontend developer at heart. The front end is the first thing a person ever touches on a website — it's what they notice, what they trust (or don't), and what makes something feel easy instead of frustrating. I genuinely love building that layer: the small interactions, the little transitions, the details most people won't consciously notice but would definitely miss if they were gone.

It's also just where my creative side gets to come out and play. Right now I'm deep in personal projects to round out my portfolio, picking up Next.js (with Angular on the someday-list), and feeding a small, growing addiction to AI tools that make my workflow faster and my output better.
